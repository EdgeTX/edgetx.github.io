# Lua LVGL New Model Wizard Developer Contest Winner

We are excited to announce the winner of the Lua LVGL New Model Wizard Developer Contest - Alexander Gnauck! As a token of appreciation for his outstanding contribution, Alexander will receive a RadioMaster TX15 MAX Black radio with internal 2.4 GHz ExpressLRS module (per his request), generously sponsored by RadioMaster. This state-of-the-art radio will be shipped worldwide free of charge directly from the RadioMaster factory to Alexander. We extend our heartfelt congratulations to Alexander Gnauck for this well-deserved win.
