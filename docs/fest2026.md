## **Event Information**

EdgeTX Fest 2026 will take place next to [Weston Park Model Airshow 2026](https://www.airshowinternational.co.uk/)<br/>

**Dates**: June 19th to 21st, 2026

**Times**

 - Friday: 10 AM until 5:30 PM
	 
 - Saturday: 10 AM until 5:30 PM with evening dusk air show from 8:30 PM to 11 PM
 
 - Sunday: 10 AM until 5:30 PM

**Location:** [Weston-under-Lizard, Shifnal TF11 8LE, United Kingdom](https://maps.app.goo.gl/bQtqLK4T26MmtbeC6)<br/>

When arriving at Weston Park please you use **The Shrewsbury Gate** as indicated below:

<img src="https://www.airshowinternational.co.uk/images/2024/Shrewsbury_gate.jpg">

**Entrance Fee:** 

For more about the tickets, please see the main event page at: [https://www.airshowinternational.co.uk/index.php/tickets](https://www.airshowinternational.co.uk/index.php/tickets)

## **Activities:**

- EdgeTX booth with demo radios – Meet the team and try out EdgeTX on a variety of radios
- Try out to control a RC operated [model forklift](https://makerworld.com/de/models/1395994-cyberbrick-official-forklift) and/or ball-maze game via EdgeTX radio (no age limit for trying both gizmos out)
- Prize drawings of the demo radios will take place at **5 PM** at each event day.

## **Special Notes:**

Filming and photographing will occur during EdgeTX Fest 2026. Participants of events and raffles agree to their picture being taken and to be used by EdgeTX and event sponsors on their website and social media.

Must be 18 or older to be eligible to win prizes. Minors need to be accompanied by an adult.

To be able to win at raffles, must be present at the time of the drawings. If your name is called and you are not there, then you will forfeit the prize and another name will be drawn.

Only a single item or a set can be won by a participant per event day. After winning the first item and by drawing the same name again, another name will be drawn.
